2x1 matrix of blocks¶

The following routines vertically merge two matrices which have consistent leading dimensions and sizes. When the matrices are distributed, their alignments must also be consistent. That is to say, if the inputs are consistent, a view of \(\begin{pmatrix} B_T \\ B_B \end{pmatrix}\) is returned.

Note

\(B_T\) and \(B_B\) must be contiguous in the sense that, if the local portion of \(B_T\) is \(m \times n\) with leading dimension ldim, then the top-left entry of \(B_B\) must occur precisely \(m\) entries after the top-left entry of \(B_T\), and both the leading dimensions and widths of the two matricees must be identical.
